🙏 AEMP Prayer

+
Heavenly Father, we Your children of the Catholic Archdiocese of Lagos entrust ourselves and the Archdiocesan Engagement for Mission Programme into Your Hands. Grant us Your wisdom and discernment, as we navigate the changes and challenges that lie ahead. Bless the work of our hands, that the AEMP may contribute to the well-being, growth and salvation of all. May we, with the help of the Holy Spirit, contribute to this endeavor with integrity, creativity, and perseverance. Grant us your Spirit of Unity. Help us to recognize and put aside our selfish desires and work together in harmony towards our common goals of spiritual-commitment and engagement. May our words be filled with love and respect, and our actions reflect Your grace. Give us courage and strength to overcome any anxieties that keep us from accepting the changes needed in this programme. Help us to move forward with peace in our hearts during and after this transition. When we are daunted by the pressures of the AEMP, help us to depend on your strength and increase our faith. We ask all these through Jesus Christ, our Lord. Amen. Our Lady, Seat of Wisdom, Pray for Us. Our Lady, Queen of Nigeria, Pray for Us. Our Lady, Queen of Peace, Pray for Us.

Curriculum

Week 1: Signature Themes
  • Self-rate 34 themes (1-3) or take Gallup assessment
  • Create Signature Themes collage for each Top 5
  • Post 1 collage + reflection per theme on WhatsApp
Week 2: Living Your Strengths
  • Read Gallup descriptions + watch theme webinars
  • 3+ examples per theme from daily life
  • New ways to use each theme (family, work, parish)
  • Post 1 theme/day (Mon-Fri)
Week 3: Developing Your Strengths
  • What if I used this more? What if less?
  • One action per theme to use it better
  • Find someone who exemplifies each theme
  • Complete daily journaling template
Week 4: Sustaining Your Strengths
  • How 5 themes work together as a team
  • Identify blind spots (overuse/misuse)
  • Strengths Partnership Plan with complementary person
  • Personal mission statement integrating Top 5
